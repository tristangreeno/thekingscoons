TERMS & CONDITIONS

We are a small family operation. We have fallen in love with both Jesus Christ and our beloved Maine coon’s. We raise our cats and kittens in our family home. We are interested in the best care for our cats, and use our profits to fund a ministry in Belize. We have only one female right now who has had her first litter. All of our kittens will be pets only. We have our kittens wormed twice and up to date on all shots (2 rounds) by our veterinarian. They are healthy and ready for their new home when we send them to you. Each cat will be around 10 to 12 weeks old when they go to their new home.

 

  

Our cats cost $850, with a non-refundable deposit of $150 (to hold our cats). We guarantee our cats for one year. We ask our owners to feed them “Royal Canin." If our cats have a health problem and pass away, we ask for a veterinarian to give a legal paper stating the cause of death. We will replace your cat if our cat death was not due to an accident or negligence.

All new owners of our kittens must sign an agreement to not declaw our cats if the cat must change ownership. Our cat will come to us for first right of refusal. Our cats will always be part of our family; we genuinely and wholeheartedly care about our cats' well being, and want to ensure that our cats will never be without a loving home.
